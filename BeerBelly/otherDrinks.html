Beck’s – Blue Alcohol-Free Beer (0.05%)

£2.00

Beck's Blue is a light, crisp and refreshing non-alcoholic alternative to beer, with no compromise on taste.

Beck’s Blue Alcohol-Free Beer (0.05%)

Golden in colour, Beck's Blue is a classic German-style pilsner lager with over 140 years of heritage. Originally brewed in Germany according to the Reinheitsgebot, the German purity law of 1516, with only 4 natural ingredients: barley, hops, yeast and water. Beck's Blue goes through the same brewing process as traditional Beck's and contains the same four natural ingredients - barley, hops, yeast and water. The only difference being that Beck's Blue undergoes de-alcoholisation, so it's alcohol free.
