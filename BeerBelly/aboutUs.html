About Us

Our mission statement at Beer Belly is to provide you, the consumer, with the greatest choices in beer. From brewery, to us and then on to you, at every stage giving you the most amount of choice and information that we can deliver. Our simple, minimalist design is al purposed so that you have the easiest time deciding which beers you want to buy. Started as a project by five computing students in West London, Beer Belly has grown into the one-stop-shop for beer purchases. We’re here to cater to your every plan, from Party to BBQ and even into Quarantine we’re be here, for you!
